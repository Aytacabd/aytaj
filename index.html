Chapter I  —  Prologue

A brief introduction

Hello, reader.

I am a Master's student at DFKI — the German Research Center for Artificial Intelligence — in Saarbrücken. Alongside my studies at Saarland University, I also work as a Junior Researcher at the August-Wilhelm Scheer Institute. I came to Germany in 2024 through a DAAD scholarship, from Baku.

Before Germany, my path crossed credit-risk modelling, financial fraud detection, and computer vision for road infrastructure. Along the way I have published eight papers and earned a few first-place awards.

I genuinely enjoy writing code — clean, thoughtful, and purposeful. The kind that makes things easier, solves real problems, or creates new possibilities. I’m always curious about what makes good systems truly work.

Outside of work, I keep a small garden, cook dishes from Azerbaijan, and read widely.
